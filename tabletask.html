Revolution 2020

"Revolution 2020" is a gripping novel written by Chetan Bhagat, centered around the lives of three friends in Varanasi, India, whose aspirations and choices are entangled in love, corruption, and the pursuit of success. Set against the backdrop of India's contemporary socio-political landscape, the book explores the complexities of ambition, relationships, and the price one must pay to achieve their dreams.

Chetan_Bhagat

Chetan Bhagat is a renowned Indian author known for his captivatipng storytelling and insightful portrayal of contemporary societal issues. With a unique blend of wit and realism, his works, including "Revolution 2020," have resonated with readers worldwide, making him one of India's most popular and influential modern writers.

It-Ends-With-Us

"It Ends with Us" is a powerful and emotionally charged novel written by Colleen Hoover, delving into the complexities of love, resilience, and personal growth. Through a gripping narrative, Hoover explores the delicate balance between heartache and hope, leaving readers with a profound understanding of the strength it takes to break free from destructive cycles.


colleen-hoover

Colleen Hoover is a critically acclaimed contemporary romance author known for her poignant and emotionally gripping storytelling. With a remarkable ability to delve into profound themes and create relatable characters, she has captured the hearts of readers worldwide, solidifying her position as a masterful storyteller in the romance genre.

Rowling, following the magical journey of a young wizard, Harry Potter, as he navigates the world of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, battling dark forces and discovering his true destiny as the Boy Who Lived.
